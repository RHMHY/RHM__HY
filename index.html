FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

How long does it take to build a website?
+
It depends on the complexity. A basic website takes 3–7 days, a business website 7–14 days, and an e-commerce site 2–4 weeks. We always provide a clear timeline before starting.
What is included in the website development cost?
+
All our packages include design, development, testing, deployment, mobile optimization, basic SEO setup, and free SSL. Specific inclusions vary by package — check our Pricing page for details.
Do you provide hosting services?
+
Yes! We offer managed cloud hosting starting from ₹999/month with free SSL, daily backups, CDN, and 99.9% uptime guarantee. We handle everything so you don't have to worry about technical details.
Can you redesign my existing website?
+
Absolutely! We specialize in website redesigns. We'll analyze your current site, understand your goals, and create a modern, high-performing version that retains your existing SEO value.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept UPI, bank transfer, Razorpay (credit/debit cards), and PayPal for international clients. We typically work on a 50% advance, 50% on delivery model.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
+
100% yes. Every website we build is fully responsive and tested on all major devices and browsers — smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Mobile-first design is our standard approach.
Do you offer SEO services after launch?
+
Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch SEO packages starting from ₹5,000/month. This includes keyword tracking, content optimization, backlink building, and monthly performance reports.
Can I update the website content myself?
+
Yes! We build websites with easy-to-use CMS (Content Management System) backends like WordPress so you can update text, images, and content without any technical knowledge. We also provide training.
Do you work with clients outside of Chennai?
+
Yes! We work with clients across India and internationally. All communication happens via WhatsApp, email, and video calls. Distance has never been an issue for delivering premium work.
What support do you offer after delivery?
+
All our packages include free support (1–6 months depending on the plan). After that, we offer affordable maintenance plans. We're always reachable via WhatsApp for urgent issues.
